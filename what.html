What We Do

Commercially viable sustainable technologies

Commercial activities in Low Earth orbit (LEO) have been rapidly increasing in recent years due to lower launch costs and cheaper, commercially available satellite hardware. This increase is unsustainable for the LEO environment, and poses a very real risk due to the rise in smallsat and cubesat constellations.

Our Capabilities

We develop sustainable end-to-end solutions for satellite operators to manage their assets more effectively in-orbit and significantly reducing their risks and liabilities.

Our technology being developed will be able to track, capture and de-orbit satellites - crucial for the services that we provide. More information on the project will be made available soon.
